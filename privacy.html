Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

STELVIO AI ("we", "us", or "our") provides an indoor cycling training platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal data when you use our service and connect with Strava.

2. Data Collection and Use

When you connect your Strava account, we access the following information to provide training analysis:

  • Profile Information: Name, Profile Picture (to identify you in the app).
  • Activity Data: Cycling logs, Power data, Heart rate, Speed (to analyze your training load and FTP).

Specific Note on Data Usage: We use this data solely to visualize your training history and calculate metrics for indoor workouts. We do not use your data to train any Artificial Intelligence (AI) or Machine Learning (ML) models. We do not sell or share your Strava data with third parties.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

We only retain data necessary for the functionality of the app. You have the right to request the deletion of your data at any time.

  • If you wish to remove your data, please disconnect the app via your Strava settings or contact us at our support email.
  • Upon request, all your synced data will be permanently deleted from our servers immediately.
